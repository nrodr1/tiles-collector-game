Tiles Collector

Rules to The Game


You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"



There are four tile patterns below:

  • - Grange
  • - Grove
  • - Pivot
  • - Terrain



By clicking on a tile, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.

You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each tile is hidden from you until you click on it.

Everytime the game restarts, the values of each tile will change.
